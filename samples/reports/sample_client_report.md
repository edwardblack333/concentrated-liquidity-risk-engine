## Decision Context

The engine reviews whether expected fee opportunity appears sufficient relative to price-path risk, range churn, and rebalancing burden. In the full model, fee coverage ratio, or FCR, is used to compare fee opportunity with the estimated cost of price movement and range repositioning. The output is decision support, not investment advice.
